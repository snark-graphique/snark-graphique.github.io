  Let $\lambda$ be a signed measure that is absolutely continuous 
  with respect to a $\sigma$-finite positive measure $\mu$. 
  Then, there exists a function $f \in L^1(\mu)$ such that 
  \[
    \lambda(E) = \int_E f \,d\mu
  \]
  for all $\mu$-measurable sets $E$.

Let $\lambda$ be a signed measure that is absolutely continuous 
with respect to a $\sigma$-finite positive measure $\mu$. 
Then, there exists a function $f \in L^1(\mu)$ such that 
\\[
  \lambda(E) = \int_E f \,d\mu
\\]
for all $\mu$-measurable sets $E$.
